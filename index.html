The Problem

Three root causes — and 90+ new clubs per month inherit all of them.

(NRR ≈ 1.0 — flat fees, no expansion path.)

Coordination Stack Stuck in 2005

  • Ratings force grandma to compete just like a pro
  • 85% play socially — matched competitively
  • They reduce everything unique about a person to one number

Perishable Inventory

  • Court utilization varies widely club to club — empty hours vanish forever
  • No system actively gets members onto empty courts
  • A member who comes one more day per week changes everything — but no one knows how to trigger it

🔍 Manual Everything

  • Scheduling, matchmaking, and calendar-building all done by hand — by one human expert per club
  • Chemistry, reliability, vibe — all trapped in that person’s head, invisible to any system
  • When that person leaves, the knowledge walks out the door
